Savoury Snacks


One of my biggest weaknesses is my craving for salty, savoury snacks because they taste so good.
Its perfect at any time of the day, but especially as a late night treat or when you're sitting down to watch a movie.
So I've compiled a list of my favorite savoury snacks that I can't get enough of.

Popcorn

Popcorn is a popular snack food at sporting events and in movie theaters, where it has been served since the 1930s.

There are various interpretations of it. You can have salty, sweet or flavoured options.

Pringles

Pringles is an American brand of stackable potato-based crisps.

Some flavors include original, salt and vinegar, sour cream and onion, cheddar cheese, ranch dressing, barbecue, hot and spicy, and loaded baked potato.

Doritos

Doritos is an American brand of flavored tortilla chips and the concept originated in a restaurant at Disneyland.

Some flavors include nacho cheese, cool ranch, spicy nacho and sour cream and onion.




Hot Pockets

Hot Pockets is an American brand of microwaveable turnovers generally containing one or more types of cheese, meat, or vegetables.

There are 50 varieties of the traditional Hot Pocket, including breakfast, lunch and dinner varieties.

Ruffles

Ruffles is an American brand of ruffled potato chips.

Ruffles are produced in a variety of flavors including: sour cream & onion, cheddar & sour cream, cheese, barbecue, salt & vinegar, cream cheese, and hot wings.

Combos

Combos, officially called Combos Stuffed Snacks, are cylindrical tubes of cracker, pretzel, or tortilla, available with various fillings.

Some combos flavors include: cheddar cheese pretzel, pizzeria pretzel and pepperoni pizza cracker.
